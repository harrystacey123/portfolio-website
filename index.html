About Harry

I am a full-stack web developer based in London with an undergraduate degree in Management. I have a passion for computers, problem solving and all things tech. I have an eager attitude with every application I build, paying close attention to detail. Additionally, I am a big advocate for collaboration – teamwork at university was crucial to success and I have definitely brought this idea forward in the way I code.

I recently graduated from General Assembly, a full-time web development course in San Francisco, USA, learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript, as well as frameworks and libraries including Node.js, React.js, and Django. I have returned to London and am seeking exciting career opportunities, with a preference for front-end roles.
